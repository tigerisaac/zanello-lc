# References

Papers consulted during the search and audit. The TeX sources of several of these
were downloaded into the working directory during the run; they are **not
redistributed in this repository** (the arXiv non-exclusive license does not grant
third-party redistribution). Links are given instead.

## The problem

- F. Zanello, *Log-concavity of level Hilbert functions and pure O-sequences*,
  [arXiv:2210.09447](https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.09447).
  Source of the open problem: states that log-concavity for codimension 3, type 2
  is "the main case that remains open … still open in any characteristic."

## The load-bearing input of the proof

- F. Zanello, *Stanley's theorem on codimension 3 Gorenstein h-vectors*,
  Proc. Amer. Math. Soc. **134** (2006), 5–8.
  [arXiv:math/0411231](https://arxiv.org/abs/math/0411231) ·
  [DOI:10.1090/S0002-9939-05-08276-6](https://doi.org/10.1090/S0002-9939-05-08276-6).
  Elementary, characteristic-free proof that codimension-3 Gorenstein h-vectors are
  SI-sequences. Its "Remark 5" is quoted from:
- R. Stanley, *Combinatorics and Commutative Algebra*, 2nd ed., Birkhäuser (1996),
  p. 67 — an elementary characteristic-free proof of that remark (via
  $A/(0:c) \cong cA$ and Macaulay's theorem applied to $A/cA$) is reproduced in the
  audit notes.
- R. Stanley, *Hilbert functions of graded algebras*, Adv. Math. **28** (1978), 57–83.
  [DOI:10.1016/0001-8708(78)90045-2](https://doi.org/10.1016/0001-8708(78)90045-2).
